The Fedora Operating System

Why it may fit your needs.

You may want to read about Fedora myths before going further.

Fedora is a Linux-based Operating System running on most computer architectures for general usage. It's also a GNU Operating System, reason for why some people may call it Fedora GNU/Linux or Fedora Linux. However, its name is just Fedora.
